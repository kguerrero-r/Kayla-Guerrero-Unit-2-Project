common misconception and information about immigration

Some of the most common misconceptions about immigration and immigrants are the following:

  • Immigrants don't pay taxes
  • Immigrants take jobs and opportunities from Americans
  • Immigrants are a drain on the U.S. economy

Problems

What effects does immigration cause?

Many people in society believe that immigrants have a negative effect. However, according to whitehouse.gov , "Immigrants also make an important conntribution to the U.S. economy." Ultimately increasing productivity and increasing the size of the labor force.

What are some solutions to these problems

What are some of the solutions to these problems? Although there is not an immediate solution, we can find temporary solutions. Such as getting educated about these subjects, and using your voice to spread awareness.
